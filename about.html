Who Is Jesse Bernstein

A Realtor Who Actually Lives the Life He Sells.

Jesse Bernstein isn't just an agent who covers Venice Beach — he's someone who chose it. He walks the same streets, shops the same markets, and experiences the same magic of Westside living that his clients are looking for. That authentic connection to the community shapes every piece of advice he gives.

With a career spanning more than a decade on the Los Angeles Westside, Jesse has guided hundreds of buyers, sellers, and investors through some of the most consequential transactions of their lives. His clients range from first-time homebuyers navigating a competitive market to seasoned investors building coastal portfolios, creative entrepreneurs seeking architectural homes, and families planting roots in one of California's most beloved beach communities.

What distinguishes Jesse isn't just market knowledge — it's the combination of that knowledge with genuine care for outcomes, a sharp eye for architecture and design, and a marketing sensibility that goes well beyond putting a listing on the MLS. He approaches each transaction as a story to be told and a strategy to be executed.

Licensed in both California and Texas, Jesse operates under the Coldwell Banker Realty banner, giving his clients access to the resources of a global network while benefiting from the responsiveness and personal attention of a trusted local advisor. He answers his own phone. He shows up. He follows through.

The Way Jesse Works

Responsive by Default

Real estate moves fast. Jesse is reachable — by call, text, or email — and responds with urgency when the moment calls for it.

Honest Counsel

Jesse will tell you what you need to hear — not just what you want to. That candor protects his clients and builds long-term relationships.

Network-First Access
