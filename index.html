Where Jesse Works Best

Westside Neighborhoods

Each neighborhood along the Westside has its own character. Jesse knows them all — their streets, their values, and their stories.

Venice Beach

Canals, walkstreets, Abbot Kinney, and beachfront compounds. The most creative, architecturally rich neighborhood in Los Angeles.

Explore Venice →

Marina del Rey

California's largest man-made small-craft harbor. Waterfront condos, marina homes, and spectacular bay views minutes from Venice.

Explore Marina →

Santa Monica

Third Street Promenade, Montana Ave, and world-class beach access — a sophisticated coastal city with some of LA's most coveted addresses.

Explore Santa Monica →

Pacific Palisades

Clifftop estates, canyon homes, and exclusive addresses where the Santa Monica Mountains meet the Pacific. Serene Westside luxury.

Explore Palisades →

Mar Vista

The Westside's most beloved neighborhood for first-time buyers and upgraders alike — great schools, strong values, and a tight-knit community feel.

Explore Mar Vista →

Playa del Rey

A quiet coastal enclave with wide beaches, bluff-top homes, and arguably the best sunsets on the Westside. An underrated gem.

Explore Playa del Rey →
