Local Expertise.

Jesse Bernstein knows Venice Beach real estate. With deep roots in the community and an eye for architectural potential, he brings a uniquely personal approach to buying and selling on the Westside.

Licensed in both California and Texas, Jesse specializes in single-family homes, income properties, and commercial real estate. As an investor himself, with several income properties in Texas and a home base in Venice Beach, he brings both personal experience and market insight to every client relationship. With deep roots in the community and a strong eye for architecture, design, and long-term potential, Jesse offers a personal, hands-on approach to buying and selling real estate. He works with creative professionals, entrepreneurs, investors, and discerning buyers seeking more than just representation. They want a trusted advisor who understands the market, the lifestyle, and the value behind a great opportunity.
