What the Numbers
Actually Mean

Venice Beach enters 2025 with historically low inventory and steady demand from both local buyers and those relocating from San Francisco, New York, and Austin. The result is a market that continues to reward sellers and challenge buyers who aren't well-prepared.

Canal properties and walk-street homes — Venice's most distinctive micro-markets — remain disproportionately competitive. When one of these comes to market, it rarely lasts more than two weeks. Off-market access is more valuable than ever.

For sellers, this is an exceptional time to list if your property is in the right condition and priced with discipline. Overpriced listings still languish — buyers are sophisticated and well-informed. The homes that are selling fast are the ones with a compelling story and strategic marketing.
