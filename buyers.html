From Search to Close

The Buying Process

01

Discovery & Alignment

Jesse starts with a real conversation — about your life, your priorities, your timeline, and your budget. The goal is to understand not just what you want in a home, but why.

02

Strategic Search

You'll get curated property alerts, off-market opportunities, and tours organized around your schedule — no wasted weekends looking at the wrong homes.

03

Competitive Offer

When you find the one, Jesse moves quickly. He analyzes the comps, reads the situation, and crafts an offer designed to win — not just compete.
