Why the Westside

Supply Is Constrained.
Demand Never Sleeps.

Venice Beach is bordered by the Pacific Ocean to the west, Santa Monica to the north, Marina del Rey to the south, and Culver City to the east. There is no more land to build. That geographic scarcity is the foundation of every investment thesis Jesse makes for this market.

Combine that scarcity with Silicon Beach's technology employment corridor, LAX proximity, strong short-term rental demand, and year-round tourism — and you have one of the most fundamentally sound coastal investment markets in the country.

Jesse has helped investors identify income properties, value-add opportunities, ADU development plays, and 1031 exchange targets across Venice, Marina del Rey, Playa Vista, Playa del Rey, and Mar Vista.

The Investment Case

Geographic Scarcity

Coastal land is finite. Venice Beach cannot sprawl outward — values have only one long-term direction.

Silicon Beach Employment

Google, Snapchat, Amazon Studios, and hundreds of tech and media companies anchor the demand for Westside housing at all price points.

Rental Demand

Both long-term and short-term rental demand is exceptionally strong, driven by visitors, remote workers, and the perpetual allure of beach living.

ADU & Value-Add Potential

California's ADU-friendly legislation creates significant income and equity-building opportunities for the right properties.

How Jesse Helps Investors

Whether you're building a portfolio or acquiring your first income property, Jesse brings analytical rigor and local insight to every decision.

01

Income Properties

Multi-unit properties, duplexes, and single-family homes with strong rental upside. Jesse helps you evaluate cap rates, cash-on-cash returns, and long-term appreciation in the context of the Venice market.

02

Value-Add Opportunities

Dated properties in exceptional locations. Jesse identifies homes where strategic renovation adds significant equity — particularly architectural properties that the market undervalues due to presentation.

03

ADU Development

California's ADU laws make many Venice Beach lots ideal for accessory dwelling unit development — a strategy that can dramatically improve cash flow and total asset value.

04

1031 Exchanges

Jesse has navigated numerous 1031 exchange transactions on the Westside, including Reverse 1031s. He connects investors with the right intermediaries and identifies replacement properties under tight timelines.

05

Off-Market Acquisition

The best investment opportunities rarely reach the MLS. Jesse's network of property owners, developers, and agents gives his investor clients first access to deals before they're publicly available.

06

Long-Term Portfolio Building

Jesse takes a long view. He helps investors acquire strategically — not just chasing today's deals, but building a coastal portfolio designed for compounding appreciation over decades.
